Frequently Asked Questions

What is WellCircle?

WellCircle is a mental health platform that provides community support, tools, and resources to enhance your well-being.

Is this therapy or professional advice?

No, WellCircle is not a substitute for professional medical or therapeutic services. It complements your journey with tools and support.

How do I talk to a counselor?

You can request to speak to a licensed professional through the “Talk to a Counselor” feature within the WellCircle app.

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page and click “Forgot Password?” to receive reset instructions via email.
